Winter Events

Ski Roundtop

Ski Roundtop is a ski resort located in Lewisberry, Pennsylvania. They offer both summer and winter experiences, though this will focus on the winter offers. During the winter, you can go to Ski Roundtop and partake in their snow sports such as skiing, snow tubing, snowboarding, and sledding. They also offer classes to learn how to ski and snowboard!

Farm Show

January also offers the annual Farm Show. The Farm Show is one of the largest agricultural expositions in the country and showcases the different parts of Pennsylvania's finest agricultural and economical facets. There are a host of food stands, crafts, and live animals to see. The Farm Show is located at the Farm Show Complex in Harrisburg.
