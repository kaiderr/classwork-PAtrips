Summer Events

Jubilee Day

Jubilee Day is the largest one day street fair on the East Coast and it's held right in Mechanicsburg, Pennsylvania. Every year on the third Thursday in June, Main Street in Mechanicsburg is closed off to host a variety of business stands, games, food trucks, and music shows. The fair has been an ongoing tradition for almost 100 years!

Hershey Park

Hershey Park is the largest theme park in Pennsylvania and attracts visitors from all over the country. Though most Central PA citizens know about the park, it is truly a hallmark destination for any vacation trip. Hershey Park unveiled a new ride last year, Wildcat's Revenge, which is a wood and steel hybrid ride. The park also offers the water park, Chocolate World, ZooAmerica, and specialty food and drinks throughout the area.
