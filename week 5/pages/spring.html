Spring Events

Maple Syrup Festival

In March, visitors can go to the Maple Syrup festival in Harrisburg! The event is all about maple syrup - how to tap trees for it, selling and buying maple snacks and food, and other activities aimed towards the family. Though there are other similar festivals all across the East Coast and Pennsylvania, this one is locally held in the state's capital.

Ice and Fire Festival

Harrisburg also hosts the Ice and Fire festival in March. This is a newer annual festival, but is having great growth in interest over the years.
