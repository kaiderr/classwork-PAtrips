Fall Events

Renaissance Faire

When the seasons change into fall, so do the festivals! In fall, the annual Renaissance Faire takes place from August until October in Manheim. Each weekend boasts a new and exciting theme, such as pirates, fairies, or wizards. It gives the goers an opportunity to dress up and enjoy the new atmosphere provided each week.

National Apple Harvest Festival

Fall is also synonymous with drinks like apple cider. The National Apple Harvest Festival is held in Biglerville every October. Everything from apple cider, apple donuts, apple pie, and much more can be found at this event. You can find locally owned businesses selling their wares and crafts, as well as the numerous food trucks and stands!
